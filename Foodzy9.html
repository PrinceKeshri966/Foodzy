Who are we

Hi ! We are a group of students who want to make both shopkeeper's and student's life easy by designing an interface where one can pre-book their order and not have to wait at the shop. We have come up with this idea because we have noticed that many students have to wait for their order at the shop for a very long time. Our idea is to solve this problem through our platform. We believe that our platform will help shopkeepers and students by providing a convenient way for them to place orders online. As a result, they will be able to increase their sales and save time.
